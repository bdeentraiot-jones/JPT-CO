Our Story

A Legacy of Trust & Financial Wisdom

Founded in the heart of Paraniputhur, Chennai, JPT & Co began as a small audit practice with a singular mission — to bring clarity, transparency, and integrity to every financial statement we touched.

Over nearly two decades, we have grown into one of Chennai's most respected audit firms, serving everyone from small proprietorships to large listed companies across Tamil Nadu and beyond.

Our team of chartered accountants and financial Chartered Accountants blends deep regulatory expertise with a practical understanding of business — ensuring your compliance is never a burden, but a competitive advantage.
